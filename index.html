About Me

I am Ishaan Zaveri, a photographer and videographer from Mumbai, India. I shoot with a Sony a7iv and I am a professional photographer and videographer. I have worked with multiple companies like Purdue University, Electrolab, Solderon and Ovenry to produce high quality videos and images for them.

Currently I am a videographer (1 of 3) for the Boiler Ambassadors team for Purdue Marketing and Communications. In this position, I have worked on many projects for Purdue including the Purdue Global Commencement (1 of 5 videographers shooting the event), Boiler Makers on the Streets (main videographer) and various other timelapses and pictures that have made it to their Instagram.   • Boiler Maker Special
